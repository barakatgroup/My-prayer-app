Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PrayerTimes Barakat (“the App”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Location Information

The App can use your device location to calculate accurate prayer times for your current area.

  • Precise location (GPS) and/or approximate location may be requested depending on your permission choice.
  • Location may be used to determine your city and country (via Android Geocoder on your device).

You can use the App without granting location permission, but accuracy may be reduced.

B. Device & App Data (Technical Data)

We may collect limited technical information needed for app functionality and analytics/ads, such as:

  • Device model, OS version, language, and app version.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad-related events (e.g., ad impressions/clicks), when ads are enabled.

C. Notifications & Alarm Scheduling Preferences

The App stores your in-app preferences locally, such as notification lead times, sound modes, and manual adjustments. These preferences are stored on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • Calculate and display prayer times.
  • Schedule local notifications/alarms.
  • Show ads (if enabled) and measure performance.
  • Improve stability and troubleshoot issues.

3. Data Sources Used by the App

To retrieve prayer times, the App may send your latitude and longitude to a third-party service: AlAdhan API (https://api.aladhan.com).

4. Advertising (Third-Party SDKs)

The App may display ads using third-party partners, including: Appodeal. These partners collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with prayer time API providers and advertising providers strictly for app functionality.

6. Data Storage & Retention

Most app data is stored locally on your device and remains there until you clear app data or uninstall the App.

7. Your Choices & Controls

You can disable location permissions, notifications, or clear local data via your device system settings at any time.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App, though no method is 100% secure.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us:
